{% raw %}<p>We present a complete proof of the exact formula for the one-point distribution for the narrow-wedge Hopf-Cole solution to the Kardar-Parisi-Zhang (KPZ) equation. This presentation is intended to be self-contained so no previous knowledge about stochastic PDEs, or exactly solvable systems is presumed.</p>{% endraw %}
