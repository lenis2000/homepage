{% raw %}<p>For temperatures below the critical temperature, the magnetic susceptibility for the two-dimensional isotropic Ising model can be expressed in terms of an infinite series of multiple integrals. With respect to a parameter related to temperature and the interaction constant, the integrals may be extended to functions analytic outside the unit circle. In a groundbreaking paper, B. G. Nickel identified a class of singularities of these integrals on the unit circle. In this note we show that there are no other singularities on the unit circle.</p>{% endraw %}
