{% raw %}<p>We construct a bijection between marked bumpless pipedreams with reverse compatible pairs, which are in bijection with not-necessarily-reduced pipedreams. This directly unifies various formulas for Grothendieck polynomials in the literature. Our bijection is a generalization of a variant of the bijection of Gao and Huang in the unmarked, reduced case.</p>{% endraw %}
