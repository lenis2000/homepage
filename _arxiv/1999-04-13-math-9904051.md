{% raw %}<p>We construct an explicit realization of a minimal representation of G, where G is the conformal group of a real Jordan algebra N. We characterize spherical vectors for these representation and prove that they are closely related to the Bessel K-function <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>K</mi><mi>τ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>z</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">K_\tau (z)</annotation></semantics></math></span>. The resulting construction can be used to study tensor powers of the minimal representation and establish an extension of the Howe duality correspondence to some exceptional groups.</p>{% endraw %}
