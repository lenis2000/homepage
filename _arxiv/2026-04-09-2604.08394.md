{% raw %}<p>Given a pair of finite posets <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>A</mi><mo>⊆</mo><mi>P</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A \subseteq P</annotation></semantics></math></span>, the function counting integer-valued order preserving extensions of an order preserving map <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>λ</mi><mo>:</mo><mi>A</mi><mo>→</mo><mi mathvariant="double-struck">Z</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\lambda: A\rightarrow \mathbb{Z}</annotation></semantics></math></span> is given by a piecewise polynomial in <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>λ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\lambda</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We provide a criterion for the nonnegativity of the coefficients of these multivariate polynomials and apply it to show that marked order polytopes of skew shapes are Ehrhart positive in a multivariate sense. This extends recent results of Ferroni-Morales-Panova on order polytopes of skew shapes and proves conjectures on the Ehrhart positivity of skew Gelfand-Tsetlin polytopes and <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>m</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">m</annotation></semantics></math></span>-generalized Pitman-Stanley polytopes due to Alexanderson-Alhajjar and Dugan-Hegarty-Morales-Raymond, respectively.</p>{% endraw %}
