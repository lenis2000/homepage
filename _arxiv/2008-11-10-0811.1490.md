{% raw %}<p>We give a geometric description of the motion of eigenvalues of a Brownian motion with values in some matrix spaces. In the second part we consider a paper by Polya where he introduced a function close to the Riemann zeta function, which satisfies Riemann hypothesis. We show that each of these two functions can be related to Brownian motion on a symmetric space.</p>{% endraw %}
