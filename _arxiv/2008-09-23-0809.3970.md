{% raw %}<p>Bleher and Kuijlaars, and Daems and Kuijlaars showed that the correlation functions of the eigenvalues of a random matrix from unitary ensemble with external source can be expressed in terms of the Christoffel-Darboux kernel for multiple orthogonal polynomials. We obtain a representation of this Christoffel-Darboux kernel in terms of the usual orthogonal polynomials.</p>{% endraw %}
