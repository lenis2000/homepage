{% raw %}<p>We analyse statistics of the real eigenvalues of gl(N,R)-valued Brownian motion (the 'Ginibre evolution') in the limit of large <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span>. In particular, we calculate the limiting two-time correlation function of spin variables associated with real eigenvalues of the Ginibre evolution. We also show how the formalism of spin variables can be used to compute the fixed time correlation functions of real eigenvalues discovered originally by Forrester and Nagao and Borodin and Sinclair.</p>{% endraw %}
