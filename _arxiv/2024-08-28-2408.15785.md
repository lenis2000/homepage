{% raw %}<p>Using a recently developed method for proving asymptotics via orthogonal polynomial duality arXiv:2305.17602, we prove that the dynamic ASEP introduced in arXiv:1701.05239 has asymptotics which are either distributed as the Tracy--Widom \(F_2,\) or are almost surely bounded. Using a different duality, we also provide contour integrals formulas for multi--species ASEP, which generalize results for the single--species ASEP.</p>{% endraw %}
