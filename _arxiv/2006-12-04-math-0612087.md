{% raw %}<p>We define the elliptic quantum group <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>E</mi><mrow><mi>τ</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>η</mi></mrow></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><msub><mi>o</mi><mn>3</mn></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">E_{\tau,\eta}(so_3)</annotation></semantics></math></span> and the transfer matrix corresponding to its simplest highest weight representation. We use Bethe anstaz method to construct the creation operators as polynomials of the Lax matrix elements expressed through a recurrence relation. We give common eigenvectors and eigenvalues of the family of commuting transfer matrices.</p>{% endraw %}
