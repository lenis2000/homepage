{% raw %}<p>Three phases of macroscopic domains have been seen for large but finite periodic dimer models; these are known as the frozen, rough and smooth phases. The transition region between the frozen and rough region has received a lot of attention for the last twenty years and recently work has been underway to understand the rough-smooth transition region in the case of the two-periodic Aztec diamond. We compute uniform asymptotics for dimer-dimer correlations of the two-periodic Aztec diamond when the dimers lie in the rough-smooth transition region. These asymptotics rely on a formula found in [5] for the inverse Kasteleyn matrix, they also apply to a related infinite dimer model.</p>{% endraw %}
