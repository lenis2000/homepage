{% raw %}<p>In earlier work the authors obtained integral formulas for probabilities for a single particle in the asymmetric simple exclusion process. Here formulas are obtained for joint probabilities for several particles. In the case of a single particle the derivation here is simpler than the one in the earlier work for one of its main results.</p>{% endraw %}
