{% raw %}<p>Our understanding of the one-dimensional KPZ equation, \textit{alias} noisy Burgers equation, has advanced substantially over the past five years. We provide a non-technical review, where we limit ourselves to the stochastic PDE and lattice type models approximating it.</p>{% endraw %}
