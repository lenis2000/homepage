{% raw %}<p>The evaluation homomorphisms from the super Yangian $\Ymn$ to the universal enveloping algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi mathvariant="normal">U</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mrow><mi mathvariant="fraktur">g</mi><mi mathvariant="fraktur">l</mi></mrow><mrow><mi>m</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>n</mi></mrow></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\U(\gl_{m|n})</annotation></semantics></math></span> allows one to regard the covariant tensor module of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mrow><mi mathvariant="fraktur">g</mi><mi mathvariant="fraktur">l</mi></mrow><mrow><mi>m</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>n</mi></mrow></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\gl_{m|n}</annotation></semantics></math></span> as $\Ymn$ modules. We study simple quotients of the submodules generated by a tensor product of highest weight vectors inside the tensor products of covariant evaluation modules. In the case <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>n</mi><mo>=</mo><mn>0</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">n=0</annotation></semantics></math></span>, this recover all finite-dimensional simple modules of $\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$. We give a necessary and sufficient condition for such modules to be tame, which generalizes the earlier work of Nazarov and Tarasov for $\Y(\mathfrak{gl}_m)$ to the super case.</p>{% endraw %}
