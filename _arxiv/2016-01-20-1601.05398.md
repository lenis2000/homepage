{% raw %}<p>This paper constructs a new interacting particle system on a two--dimensional lattice with geometric jumps near a boundary which partially reflects the particles. The projection to each horizontal level is Markov, and on every level the dynamics match stochastic matrices constructed from pure alpha characters of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>p</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi mathvariant="normal">∞</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">Sp(\infty)</annotation></semantics></math></span>, while on every other level they match an interacting particle system from Pieri formulas for <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>p</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mn>2</mn><mi>r</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">Sp(2r)</annotation></semantics></math></span>. Using a previously discovered correlation kernel, asymptotics are shown to be the Discrete Jacobi and Symmetric Pearcey processes.</p>{% endraw %}
