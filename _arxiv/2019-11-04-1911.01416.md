{% raw %}<p>We study the solution to a nonlinear stochastic heat equation in <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>d</mi><mo>≥</mo><mn>3</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">d\geq 3</annotation></semantics></math></span>. The equation is driven by a Gaussian multiplicative noise that is white in time and smooth in space. For a small coupling constant, we prove (i) the solution converges to the stationary distribution in large time; (ii) the diffusive scale fluctuations are described by the Edwards-Wilkinson equation.</p>{% endraw %}
