{% raw %}<p>In this paper, we study the asymptotic behaviour of plane partitions distributed according to a weighted <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>q</mi><mrow><mi>V</mi><mi>o</mi><mi>l</mi><mi>u</mi><mi>m</mi><mi>e</mi></mrow></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q^{Volume}</annotation></semantics></math></span> Muttalib--Borodin ensemble. Specifically, we establish a Large Deviation principle for the discrete Muttalib--Borodin process, characterising the rate function. Furthermore, through Riemann--Hilbert analysis, we give an explicit expression for the asymptotic shape of the partition.</p>{% endraw %}
