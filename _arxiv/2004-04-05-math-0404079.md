{% raw %}<p>We consider the coincident root loci consisting of the polynomials with at least two double roots andpresent a linear basis of the corresponding ideal in the algebra of symmetric polynomials in terms of the Jack polynomials with special value of parameter <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>α</mi><mo>=</mo><mo>−</mo><mn>2.</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\alpha= -2.</annotation></semantics></math></span> As a corollary we present an explicit formula for the Hilbert-Poincarè series of this ideal and the generator of the minimal degree as a special Jack polynomial. A generalization to the case of the symmetric polynomials vanishing on the double shifted diagonals and the Macdonald polynomials specialized at <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>t</mi><mn>2</mn></msup><mi>q</mi><mo>=</mo><mn>1</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">t^2 q = 1</annotation></semantics></math></span> is also presented. We also give similar results for the interpolation Jack polynomials.</p>{% endraw %}
