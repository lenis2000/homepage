{% raw %}<p>Let A_q(g) be the quantized algebra of functions associated with simple Lie algebra g defined by generators obeying the so called RTT relations. We describe the embedding <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>A</mi><mi>q</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>B</mi><mn>2</mn></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo><mo>↪</mo><msub><mi>A</mi><mi>q</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>C</mi><mn>2</mn></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A_q(B_2) \hookrightarrow A_q(C_2)</annotation></semantics></math></span> explicitly. As an application, a new solution of the Isaev-Kulish 3D reflection equation is constructed by combining the embedding with the previous solution for A_q(C_2) by the authors.</p>{% endraw %}
