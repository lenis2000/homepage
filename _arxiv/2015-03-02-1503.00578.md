{% raw %}<p>We investigate the global fluctuations of solutions to elliptic equations with random coefficients in the discrete setting. In dimension <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>d</mi><mo>≥</mo><mn>3</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">d\geq 3</annotation></semantics></math></span> and for i.i.d.\ coefficients, we show that after a suitable scaling, these fluctuations converge to a Gaussian field that locally resembles a (generalized) Gaussian free field. The paper begins with a heuristic derivation of the result, which can be read independently and was obtained jointly with Scott Armstrong.</p>{% endraw %}
