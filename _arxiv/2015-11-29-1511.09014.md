{% raw %}<p>We construct a monomorphism of the De Rham complex of scalar multivalued meromorphic forms on the projective line, holomorphic on the complement to a finite set of points, to the chain complex of the Lie algebra of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>s</mi><msub><mi>l</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">sl_2</annotation></semantics></math></span>-valued algebraic functions on the same complement with coefficients in a tensor product of contragradient Verma modules over the affine Lie algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mover accent="true"><mrow><mi>s</mi><mi>l</mi></mrow><mo>^</mo></mover><mn>2</mn></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\hat{sl}_2</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We show that the existence of singular vectors in the Verma modules (the Malikov-Feigin-Fuchs singular vectors) is reflected in the new relations between the cohomology classes of logarithmic differential forms.</p>{% endraw %}
