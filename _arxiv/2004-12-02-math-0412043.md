{% raw %}<p>In this series of articles we study connections between combinatorics of multidimensional generalizations of Cauchy identity and continuous objects such as multidimensional Brownian motions and Brownian bridges. In Part I of the series we present a bijective proof of multidimensional generalizations of the Cauchy identity. Our bijection uses oriented planar trees equipped with some linear orders.</p>{% endraw %}
