{% raw %}<p>We consider the local field dynamical temperature correlation function of the Quantum Nonlinear Schrodinger equation with the finite coupling constant. This correlation function admits a Fredholm determinant representation. The related operator-valued Riemann--Hilbert problem is used for analysing the leading term of the large time and long distance asymptotics of the correlation function.</p>{% endraw %}
