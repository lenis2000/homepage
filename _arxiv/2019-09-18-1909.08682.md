{% raw %}<p>The main result of this paper is the construction of a family of superintegrable Hamiltonian systems on moduli spaces of flat connections on a principle <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>G</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">G</annotation></semantics></math></span>-bundle on a surface. The moduli space is a Poisson variety with Atiyah-Bott Poisson structure. Among particular cases of such systems are spin generalizations of Ruijsenaars-Schneider models.</p>{% endraw %}
