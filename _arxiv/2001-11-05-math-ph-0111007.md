{% raw %}<p>The authors show that a wide class of Fredholm determinants arising in the representation theory of "big" groups such as the infinite-dimensional unitary group, solve Painleve equations. Their methods are based on the theory of integrable operators and the theory of Riemann-Hilbert problems.</p>{% endraw %}
