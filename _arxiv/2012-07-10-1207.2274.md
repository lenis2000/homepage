{% raw %}<p>We consider the population of critical points generated from the trivial critical point of the master function with no variables and associated with the trivial representation of the affine Lie algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mover accent="true"><mrow><mi mathvariant="fraktur">s</mi><mi mathvariant="fraktur">l</mi></mrow><mo>^</mo></mover><mi>N</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\hat{\frak{sl}}_N</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We show that the critical points of this population define rational solutions of the equations of the mKdV hierarchy associated with <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mover accent="true"><mrow><mi mathvariant="fraktur">s</mi><mi mathvariant="fraktur">l</mi></mrow><mo>^</mo></mover><mi>N</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\hat{\frak{sl}}_N</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We also construct critical points from suitable <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span>-tuples of tau-functions. The construction is based on a Wronskian identity for tau-functions. In particular, we construct critical points from suitable <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span>-tuples of Schur polynomials and prove a Wronskian identity for Schur polynomials.</p>{% endraw %}
