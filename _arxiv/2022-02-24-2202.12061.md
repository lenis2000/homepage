It allows a formulation in terms of the Coxeter group <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A_3</annotation></semantics></math></span>.
