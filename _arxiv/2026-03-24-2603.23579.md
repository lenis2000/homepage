{% raw %}<p>Matrix valued (asymmetric) truncated Toeplitz operators are generally not complex symmetric. In this paper, we define a new conjugation with unique properties and study its relation to matrix valued asymmetric truncated Toeplitz operators. We also explore the connections between matrix valued asymmetric truncated Toeplitz operators and Hankel operators with matrix symbols.</p>{% endraw %}
