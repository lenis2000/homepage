{% raw %}<p>We consider the totally nonfree (TNF) action of a groups and the corresponding adjoint invariant (AD) measures on the lattices of the subgroups of the given group. The main result is the description of all adjoint-invariant and TNF-measures on the lattice of subgroups of the infinite symmetric group <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>S</mi><mi mathvariant="double-struck">N</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">S_{\Bbb N}</annotation></semantics></math></span>. The problem is closely related to the theory of characters and factor representations of groups.</p>{% endraw %}
