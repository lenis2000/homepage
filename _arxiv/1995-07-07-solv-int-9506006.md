{% raw %}<p>For a particular class of integral operators <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>K</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">K</annotation></semantics></math></span> we show that the quantity \[\ph:=\log \det (I+K)-\log \det (I-K)\] satisfies both the integrated mKdV hierarchy and the sinh-Gordon hierarchy. This proves a conjecture of Zamolodchikov.</p>{% endraw %}
