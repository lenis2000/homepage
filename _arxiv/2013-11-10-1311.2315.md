{% raw %}<p>We construct approximate transport maps for non-critical Beta-matrix models, that is, maps so that the push forward of a non-critical Beta-matrix model with a given potential is a non-critical Beta-matrix model with another potential, up to a small error in the total variation distance. As a consequence, we deduce that local statistics have the same asymptotic behavior for both models.</p>{% endraw %}
