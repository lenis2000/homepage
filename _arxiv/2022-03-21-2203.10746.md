{% raw %}<p>We prove that the BGW and HCIZ integrals admit large N topological expansions for complex coupling and complex external fields, provided the coupling is sufficiently strong. The expansion coefficients are holomorphic functions which are genus-specific generating functions for the monotone single and double Hurwitz numbers, respectively.</p>{% endraw %}
