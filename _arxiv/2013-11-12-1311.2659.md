{% raw %}<p>We revisit the Helmholts equation in a quarter-plane in the framework of the Riemann-Hilbert approach to linear boundary value problems suggested in late 90s by A. Fokas. We show the role of the Sommerfeld radiation condition in Fokas's scheme.</p>{% endraw %}
