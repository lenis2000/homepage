{% raw %}<p>We derive simple linear, inhomogeneous recurrences for the variance of the index by utilising the fact that the generating function for the distribution of the number of positive eigenvalues of a Gaussian unitary ensemble is a <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>τ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\tau</annotation></semantics></math></span>-function of the fourth Painlev\'e equation. From this we deduce a simple summation formula, several integral representations and finally an exact hypergeometric function evaluation for the variance.</p>{% endraw %}
