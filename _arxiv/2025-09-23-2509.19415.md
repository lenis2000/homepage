{% raw %}<p>We show that the spatial increments of the KPZ fixed point starting from arbitrary initial data, exhibit strong quantitative comparison against rate two Brownian motion on compacts. The above estimates are uniform for uniformly bounded continuous, compactly supported initial data and countably many narrow wedges with supports contained in a fixed compact set.</p>{% endraw %}
