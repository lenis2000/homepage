{% raw %}<p>We relate a continuous-time version of the geometric RSK correspondence to the Toda lattice, in a way which can be viewed as a semi-classical limit of a recent result by the author which relates the continuous-time geometric RSK mapping, with Brownian motion as input, to the quantum Toda lattice.</p>{% endraw %}
