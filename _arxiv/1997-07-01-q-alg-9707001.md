{% raw %}<p>The theory of non-symmetric Jack polynomials is developed independently of the theory of symmetric Jack polynomials, and this theory together with the relationship between the non-symmetric, symmetric and anti-symmetric Jack polynomials is used to deduce the corresponding results for the symmetric Jack polynomials.</p>{% endraw %}
