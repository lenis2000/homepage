{% raw %}<p>We study the asymptotics of the global fluctuations for the difference between two adjacent levels in the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>β</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\beta</annotation></semantics></math></span>--Jacobi corners process (multilevel and general <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>β</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\beta</annotation></semantics></math></span> extension of the classical Jacobi ensemble of random matrices). The limit is identified with the derivative of the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>2</mn><mi>d</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">2d</annotation></semantics></math></span> Gaussian Free Field. Our main tools are integral forms for the (Macdonald-type) difference operators originating from the shuffle algebra.</p>{% endraw %}
