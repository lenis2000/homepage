{% raw %}<p>The probability that all eigenvalues of a product of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>m</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">m</annotation></semantics></math></span> independent <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi><mo>×</mo><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N \times N</annotation></semantics></math></span> sub-blocks of a Haar distributed random real orthogonal matrix of size <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>L</mi><mi>i</mi></msub><mo>+</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo><mo>×</mo><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>L</mi><mi>i</mi></msub><mo>+</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">(L_i+N) \times (L_i+N)</annotation></semantics></math></span>, <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>i</mi><mo>=</mo><mn>1</mn><mo separator="true">,</mo><mo>…</mo><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>m</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">(i=1,\dots,m)</annotation></semantics></math></span> are real is calculated as a multi-dimensional integral, and as a determinant. Both involve Meijer G-functions. Evaluation formulae of the latter, based on a recursive scheme, allow it to be proved that for any <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>m</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">m</annotation></semantics></math></span> and with each <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>L</mi><mi>i</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">L_i</annotation></semantics></math></span> even the probability is a rational number. The formulae furthermore provide for explicit computation in small order cases.</p>{% endraw %}
