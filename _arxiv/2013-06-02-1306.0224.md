{% raw %}<p>In this paper, we construct the q-analogue of Poirier-Reutenauer algebras, related deeply with other q-combinatorial Hopf algebras. As an application, we use them to realize the odd Schur functions defined in \cite{EK}, and naturally obtain the odd Littlewood-Richardson rule concerned in \cite{Ell}. Moreover, we construct the refinement of the odd Schur functions, called odd quasisymmetric Schur functions, parallel to the consideration in \cite{HLMW1}. All the q-Hopf algebras we discuss here provide the corresponding q-dual graded graphs in the sense of \cite{BLL}.</p>{% endraw %}
