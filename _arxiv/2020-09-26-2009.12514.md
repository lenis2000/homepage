{% raw %}<p>We analyze the fluctuations of the free energy, replica overlaps, and overlap with the external field in the quadratic spherical SK model with a magnetic field. We identify several different behaviors for these quantities depending on the size of the magnetic field, confirming predictions by Fyodorov-Le Doussal and recent work of Baik, Collins-Wildman, Le Doussal and Wu.</p>{% endraw %}
