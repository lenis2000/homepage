{% raw %}<p>The explicit expression for the the probability distribution function of the endpoint fluctuations of one-dimensional directed polymers in random potential is derived in terms of the Bethe ansatz replica technique by mapping the replicated problem to the N-particle quantum boson system with attractive interactions.</p>{% endraw %}
