{% raw %}<p>This paper deals with symmetric random matrices whose upper diagonal entries are obtained from a linear random field with heavy tailed noise. It is shown that the maximum eigenvalue and the spectral radius of such a random matrix with dependent entries converge to the Frech\'et distribution after appropriate scaling. This extends a seminal result of Soshnikov(2004) when the tail index is strictly less than one.</p>{% endraw %}
