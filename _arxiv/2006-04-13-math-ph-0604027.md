{% raw %}<p>The gap probabilities at the hard and soft edges of scaled random matrix ensembles with orthogonal symmetry are known in terms of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>τ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\tau</annotation></semantics></math></span>-functions. Extending recent work relating to the soft edge, it is shown that these <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>τ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\tau</annotation></semantics></math></span>-functions, and their generalizations to contain a generating function parameter, can be expressed as Fredholm determinants. These same Fredholm determinants also occur in exact expressions for gap probabilities in scaled random matrix ensembles with unitary and symplectic symmetry.</p>{% endraw %}
