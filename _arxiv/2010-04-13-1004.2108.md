{% raw %}<p>This paper describes irreducible representations in category O of the rational Cherednik algebra H_c(H_3,h) associated to the exceptional Coxeter group H_3 and any complex parameter c. We compute the characters of all these representations explicitly. As a consequence, we classify all the finite dimensional irreducible representations of H_c(H_3,h).</p>{% endraw %}
