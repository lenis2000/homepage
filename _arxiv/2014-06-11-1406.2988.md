{% raw %}<p>We present several upper and lower bounds on the Kronecker coefficients of the symmetric group. We prove <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>k</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">k</annotation></semantics></math></span>-stability of the Kronecker coefficients generalizing the (usual) stability, and giving a new upper bound. We prove a lower bound via the characters of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>n</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">S_n</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We apply these and other results to generalize Sylvester's unimodality of the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-binomial coefficients <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mrow><mo fence="true">(</mo><mfrac linethickness="0px"><mi>n</mi><mi>k</mi></mfrac><mo fence="true">)</mo></mrow><mi>q</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\binom{n}{k}_q</annotation></semantics></math></span> as polynomials in <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>: we derive explicit sharp bounds on the differences of their consecutive coefficients.</p>{% endraw %}
