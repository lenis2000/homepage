{% raw %}<p>We introduce a multi-parameter generalization of the Lambda-determinant of Robbins and Rumsey, based on the cluster algebra with coefficients attached to a T-system recurrence. We express the result as a weighted sum over alternating sign matrices.</p>{% endraw %}
