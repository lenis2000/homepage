{% raw %}<p>We show how to compute the probability of any given local configuration in a random tiling of the plane with dominos. That is, we explicitly compute the measures of cylinder sets for the measure of maximal entropy <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>μ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\mu</annotation></semantics></math></span> on the space of tilings of the plane with dominos. We construct a measure <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>ν</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nu</annotation></semantics></math></span> on the set of lozenge tilings of the plane, show that its entropy is the topological entropy, and compute explicitly the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>ν</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nu</annotation></semantics></math></span>-measures of cylinder sets. As applications of these results, we prove that the translation action is strongly mixing for <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>μ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\mu</annotation></semantics></math></span> and <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>ν</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nu</annotation></semantics></math></span>, and compute the rate of convergence to mixing (the correlation between distant events). For the measure <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>ν</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nu</annotation></semantics></math></span> we compute the variance of the height function.</p>{% endraw %}
