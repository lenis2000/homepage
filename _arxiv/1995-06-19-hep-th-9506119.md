{% raw %}<p>We present a Baxterization of a two-colour generalization of the Birman--Wenzl--Murakami (BWM) algebra. Appropriately combining two RSOS-type representations of the ordinary BWM algebra, we construct representations of the two-colour algebra. Using the Baxterization, this provides new RSOS-type solutions to the Yang--Baxter equation.</p>{% endraw %}
