{% raw %}<p>Polynomial-in-time algorithms for computing classical Hurwitz numbers were given in [4] based on the Pandharipande equation. The paritition function of double Hurwitz numbers was proved [21] to satisfy the 2-Toda hierarchy. In this paper, similar to [21] we derive Pandharipande-type equations for double Hurwitz numbers from 2-Toda hierarchy. Based on these equations and a method from [4], we study large genus as well as large degree asymptotics of double Hurwitz numbers.</p>{% endraw %}
