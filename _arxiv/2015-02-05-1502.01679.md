{% raw %}<p>MacMahon proved a simple product formula for the generating function of plane partitions fitting in a given box. The theorem implies a <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-enumeration of lozenge tilings of a semi-regular hexagon on the triangular lattice. In this paper we generalize MacMahon's classical theorem by <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-enumerating lozenge tilings of a new family of hexagons with four adjacent triangles removed from their boundary.</p>{% endraw %}
