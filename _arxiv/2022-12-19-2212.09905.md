{% raw %}<p>We consider the inhomogeneous stochastic six vertex model with periodicity starting from step initial data. We prove that it converges almost surely to a deterministic limit shape. For the proof, we map the stochastic six vertex model to a deformed version of the discrete Hammersley process. Then we construct a colored version of the model and apply Liggett's superadditive ergodic theorem. The construction of the colored model includes a new idea using a Boolean-type product, which generalizes and simplifies the method used in arXiv:2204.11158.</p>{% endraw %}
