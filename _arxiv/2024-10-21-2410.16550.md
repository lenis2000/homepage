{% raw %}<p>We extend the construction of the semigroup of the two-dimensional delta-Bose gas in Gu, Quastel, and Tsai (2021) (based on Rajeev (1999) and Dimock and Rajeev (2004)) to a weighted <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>L</mi><mn>2</mn></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">L^2</annotation></semantics></math></span> space that allows exponentially growing functions. We further show that the semigroup of the mollified delta-Bose gas converges strongly to that of the delta-Bose gas.</p>{% endraw %}
