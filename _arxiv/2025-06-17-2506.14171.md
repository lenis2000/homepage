{% raw %}<p>We provide a basis transformation that inverts the coordinate Bethe Ansatz. It is widely believed that the Bethe Ansatz is complete, based on numerical evidence and combinatorial arguments. We present a constructive and comprehensive approach to show that the Bethe Ansatz is complete. The transformation formula is verified numerically with excellent accuracy for non-zero anisotropy parameters and rings of odd length. Additionally, assuming the Bethe Ansatz is complete, we derive a novel exact formula for the one-point function systems through special identities for the Izergin-Korepin determinant.</p>{% endraw %}
