{% raw %}<p>In random tiling and dimer models we can get various limit shapes which gives the boundaries between different types of phases. The shape fluctuations at these boundaries give rise to universal limit laws, in particular the Airy process. We survey some models which can be analyzed in detail based on the fact that they are determinantal point processes with correlation kernels that can be computed. We also discuss which type of limit laws that can be obtained.</p>{% endraw %}
