{% raw %}<p>Using Riemann-Hilbert methods, we establish a Tracy-Widom like formula for the generating function of the occupancy numbers of the Pearcey process. This formula is linked to a coupled vector differential equation of order three. We also obtain a non linear coupled heat equation. Combining these two equations we obtain a PDE for the logarithm of the the generating function of the Pearcey process.</p>{% endraw %}
