{% raw %}<p>We show how to determine the asymptotics of a certain Selberg-type integral by means of tools available in the theory of (generalised) hypergeometric series. This provides an alternative derivation of a result of Carr\'e, Deneufch\^atel, Luque and Vivo [arXiv:1003.5996].</p>{% endraw %}
