{% raw %}<p>We present an exact solution for an asymmetric exclusion process on a ring with three classes of particles and vacancies. Using a matrix product Ansatz, we find explicit expressions for the weights of the configurations in the stationary state. The solution involves tensor products of quadratic algebras.</p>{% endraw %}
