{% raw %}<p>We construct an irreversible local Markov dynamics on configurations of up-right paths on a discrete two-dimensional torus, that preserves the Gibbs measures for the six vertex model. An additional feature of the dynamics is a conjecturally nontrivial drift of the height function.</p>{% endraw %}
