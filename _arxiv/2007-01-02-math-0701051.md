{% raw %}<p>We prove an upper bound for characters of the symmetric groups. Namely, we show that there exists a constant a>0 with a property that for every Young diagram \lambda with n boxes, r(\lambda) rows and c(\lambda) columns |Tr \rho^\lambda(\pi) / Tr \rho^\lambda(e)| < [a max(r(\lambda)/n, c(\lambda)/n,|\pi|/n) ]^{|\pi|}, where |\pi| is the minimal number of factors needed to write \pi\in S_n as a product of transpositions. We also give uniform estimates for the error term in the Vershik-Kerov's and Biane's character formulas and give a new formula for free cumulants of the transition measure.</p>{% endraw %}
