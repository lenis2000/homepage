{% raw %}<p>Random Matrix theory has become a field on its own with a breadth of new results, techniques, and ideas in the last thirty years. In these proceedings of the 8ECM 2021, I illustrate some of these advances by describing what is known about the spectrum and the eigenvectors of Bernoulli matrices.</p>{% endraw %}
