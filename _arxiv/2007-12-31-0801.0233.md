{% raw %}<p>The product of any finite number of factorial Schur functions can be expanded as a <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>Z</mi><mo stretchy="false">[</mo><mi>y</mi><mo stretchy="false">]</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">Z[y]</annotation></semantics></math></span>-linear combination of Schur functions. We give a rule for computing the coefficients in such an expansion which generalizes a specialization of the Molev-Sagan rule, which in turn generalizes the classical Littlewood-Richardson rule.</p>{% endraw %}
