{% raw %}<p>This paper investigates the asymptotics of eigenstructure of sample covariance matrix under the spiked covariance matrix model in ultra-high-dimensional settings, where the dimensionality can grow much faster than the sample size with <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>p</mi><mo>≍</mo><msup><mi>n</mi><mi>α</mi></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex"> p \asymp n^\alpha </annotation></semantics></math></span>, <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>α</mi><mo>&gt;</mo><mn>1</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex"> \alpha&gt; 1 </annotation></semantics></math></span>. We establish the first-order convergence limits of eigenvalue locations and eigenvector projections of properly scaled sample covariance matrix. Our results are extensions of \cite{bloemendal16,ding21}.</p>{% endraw %}
