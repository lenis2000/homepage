In particular, we obtain the asymptotic expansion up to terms <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>o</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mn>1</mn><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">o(1)</annotation></semantics></math></span>, in the large deviation regime outside the limiting interval of support.
