{% raw %}<p>We show that the emptiness formation probability of the six-vertex model with domain wall boundary conditions at its free-fermion point is a <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>τ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\tau</annotation></semantics></math></span>-function of the sixth Painlev\'e equation. Using this fact we derive asymptotics of the emptiness formation probability in the thermodynamic limit.</p>{% endraw %}
