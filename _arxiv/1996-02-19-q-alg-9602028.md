{% raw %}<p>We consider remarkable central elements of the universal enveloping algebra of the general linear algebra which we call quantum immanants. We express them in terms of generators <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>E</mi><mrow><mi>i</mi><mi>j</mi></mrow></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">E_{ij}</annotation></semantics></math></span> and as differential operators on the space of matrices. These expressions are a direct generalization of the classical Capelli identities. They result in many nontrivial properties of quantum immanants.</p>{% endraw %}
