{% raw %}<p>We study the asymptotic behavior of the discrete analogue of the holomorphic map <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>z</mi><mi>a</mi></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">z^a</annotation></semantics></math></span>. The analysis is based on the use of the Riemann-Hilbert approach. Specifically, using the Deift-Zhou nonlinear steepest descent method we prove the asymptotic formulae which was conjectured in 2000 by the first co-author and S.I.~Agafonov.</p>{% endraw %}
