{% raw %}<p>We treat the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span>-particle ZRP whose jumping rates satisfy a certain condition. This condition is required to use the Bethe ansatz and the resulting model is the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-boson model that appeared in [J. Phys. A, \textbf{31} 6057--6071 (1998)] by Sasamoto and Wadati or the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-TAZRP in \textit{MacDonald processes} by Borodin and Corwin. We find the explicit formula of the transition probability of the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-TAZRP via the Bethe ansatz. By using the transition probability we find the probability distribution of the left-most particle's position at time <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>t</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">t</annotation></semantics></math></span>. To find the probability for the left-most particle's position we find a new identity corresponding to Tracy and Widom's identity for the ASEP in [Commun. Math. Phys., \textbf{279} 815--844 (2008)]. For the initial state that all particles occupy a single site, the probability distribution of the left-most particle's position at time <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>t</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">t</annotation></semantics></math></span> is represented by the contour integral of a determinant.</p>{% endraw %}
