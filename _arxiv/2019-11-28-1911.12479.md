{% raw %}<p>By combining the Gessel--Xin method with plethystic substitutions, we obtain a recursion for a symmetric function generalization of the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-Dyson constant term identity also known as the Zeilberger--Bressoud <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-Dyson theorem. This yields a constant term identity which generalizes the non-zero part of Kadell's orthogonality ex-conjecture and a result of K\'{a}rolyi, Lascoux and Warnaar.</p>{% endraw %}
