{% raw %}<p>Gustafson and Milne proved an identity on the Schur function indexed by a partition of the form <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>λ</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo>−</mo><mi>n</mi><mo>+</mo><mi>k</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>λ</mi><mn>2</mn></msub><mo>−</mo><mi>n</mi><mo>+</mo><mi>k</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mo>…</mo><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>λ</mi><mi>k</mi></msub><mo>−</mo><mi>n</mi><mo>+</mo><mi>k</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">(\lambda_1-n+k,\lambda_2-n+k,\ldots,\lambda_k-n+k)</annotation></semantics></math></span>. On the other hand, Feh\'{e}r, N\'{e}methi and Rim\'{a}nyi found an identity on the Schur function indexed by a partition of the form <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>m</mi><mo>−</mo><mi>k</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mo>…</mo><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>m</mi><mo>−</mo><mi>k</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>λ</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo separator="true">,</mo><mo>…</mo><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>λ</mi><mi>k</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">(m-k,\ldots,m-k, \lambda_1,\ldots,\lambda_k)</annotation></semantics></math></span>. Feh\'{e}r, N\'{e}methi and Rim\'{a}nyi gave a geometric explanation of their identity, and they raised the question of finding a combinatorial proof. In this paper, we establish a Gustafson-Milne type identity as well as a Feh\'{e}r-N\'{e}methi-Rim\'{a}nyi type identity for factorial Grothendieck polynomials. Specializing a factorial Grothendieck polynomial to a Schur function, we obtain a combinatorial proof of the Feh\'{e}r-N\'{e}methi-Rim\'{a}nyi identity.</p>{% endraw %}
