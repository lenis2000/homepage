{% raw %}<p>Eigenvalues of the commuting family of transfer matrices are expected to obey the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>T</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">T</annotation></semantics></math></span>-system, a set of functional relation, proposed recently. Here we obtain the solution to the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>T</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">T</annotation></semantics></math></span>-system for <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msubsup><mi>C</mi><mn>2</mn><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mn>1</mn><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow></msubsup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">C^{(1)}_2</annotation></semantics></math></span> vertex models. They are compatible with the analytic Bethe ansatz and Yang-Baxterize the classical characters.</p>{% endraw %}
