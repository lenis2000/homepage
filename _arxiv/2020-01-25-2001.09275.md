{% raw %}<p>In this note, we study the hyperbolic stochastic damped sine-Gordon equation (SdSG), with a parameter <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>β</mi><mn>2</mn></msup><mo>&gt;</mo><mn>0</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\beta^2 &gt; 0</annotation></semantics></math></span>, and its associated Gibbs dynamics on the two-dimensional torus. After introducing a suitable renormalization, we first construct the Gibbs measure in the range <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>0</mn><mo>&lt;</mo><msup><mi>β</mi><mn>2</mn></msup><mo>&lt;</mo><mn>4</mn><mi>π</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">0&lt;\beta^2&lt;4\pi</annotation></semantics></math></span> via the variational approach due to Barashkov-Gubinelli (2018). We then prove almost sure global well-posedness and invariance of the Gibbs measure under the hyperbolic SdSG dynamics in the range <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>0</mn><mo>&lt;</mo><msup><mi>β</mi><mn>2</mn></msup><mo>&lt;</mo><mn>2</mn><mi>π</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">0&lt;\beta^2&lt;2\pi</annotation></semantics></math></span>. Our construction of the Gibbs measure also yields almost sure global well-posedness and invariance of the Gibbs measure for the parabolic sine-Gordon model in the range <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>0</mn><mo>&lt;</mo><msup><mi>β</mi><mn>2</mn></msup><mo>&lt;</mo><mn>4</mn><mi>π</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">0&lt;\beta^2&lt;4\pi</annotation></semantics></math></span>.</p>{% endraw %}
