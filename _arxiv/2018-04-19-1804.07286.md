We also prove that the scaling limit of counting measure on the pivotal points, which was proved to exist by Garban, Pete, and Schramm (2013), is the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>3</mn><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mn>4</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">3/4</annotation></semantics></math></span>-dimensional Minkowski content up to a deterministic multiplicative constant.</p>{% endraw %}
