{% raw %}<p>We study the stability of the mesoscopic fluctuations of certain orthogonal polynomial ensembles on the real line utilizing the recurrence relation of the associated orthogonal polynomials. We prove that under a sparse enough decaying perturbation of the recurrence coefficients the limiting distribution is stable.
