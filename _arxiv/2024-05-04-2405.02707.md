{% raw %}<p>While the currents of the defocusing Ablowitz-Ladik chain are unambiguously defined, no explicit formulas for their densities have been obtained yet. We close this gap. As a consequence, we also complete the argument which establishes the anticipated expression for average currents, averaged over a generalized Gibbs ensemble.</p>{% endraw %}
