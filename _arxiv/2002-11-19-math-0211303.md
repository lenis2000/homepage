{% raw %}<p>We consider biased random walk on supercritical percolation clusters in <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi mathvariant="script">Z</mi><mn>2</mn></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\Z^2</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We show that the random walk is transient and that there are two speed regimes: If the bias is large enough, the random walk has speed zero, while if the bias is small enough, the speed of the random walk is positive.</p>{% endraw %}
