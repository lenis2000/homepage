{% raw %}<p>In this paper we derive intertwining relations for a broad class of conservative particle systems both in discrete and continuous setting. Using the language of point process theory, we are able to derive a natural framework in which duality and intertwining can be formulated. We prove falling factorial and orthogonal polynomial intertwining relations in a general setting. These intertwinings unite the previously found classical and orthogonal self-dualities in the context of discrete particle systems and provide new dualities for several interacting systems in the continuum. We also introduce a new process, the symmetric inclusion process in the continuum, for which our general method applies and yields generalized Meixner polynomials as orthogonal self-intertwiners.</p>{% endraw %}
