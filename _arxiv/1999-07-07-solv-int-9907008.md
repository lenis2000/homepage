{% raw %}<p>We consider the following problem: When do alternate eigenvalues taken from a matrix ensemble themselves form a matrix ensemble? More precisely, we classify all weight functions for which alternate eigenvalues from the corresponding orthogonal ensemble form a symplectic ensemble, and similarly classify those weights for which alternate eigenvalues from a union of two orthogonal ensembles forms a unitary ensemble.
