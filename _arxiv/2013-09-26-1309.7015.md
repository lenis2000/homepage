{% raw %}<p>We consider the gap probability for the Generalized Bessel process in the single-time and multi-time case. We prove that the scalar and matrix Fredholm determinants of such process can be expressed in terms of determinants of Its-Izergin-Korepin-Slavnov integrable kernels and thus related to suitable Riemann-Hilbert problems. In the single-time case, we construct a Lax pair formalism, while in the multi-time case we explicitly define a new multi-time kernel to study.</p>{% endraw %}
