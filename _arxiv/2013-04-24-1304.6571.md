{% raw %}<p>In this brief technical communication it is demonstrated how using Bethe ansatz technique the explicit expression for the two-point free energy distribution function in (1+1) directed polymers can be derived in rather simple way. Obtained result is equivalent to the one derived earlier by Prolhac and Spohn.</p>{% endraw %}
