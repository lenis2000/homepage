{% raw %}<p>The U_q(\hat{sl}(2)) Bethe equation is studied at q=0. A linear congruence equation is proposed related to the string solutions. The number of its off-diagonal solutions is expressed in terms of an explicit combinatorial formula and coincides with the weight multiplicities of the quantum space.</p>{% endraw %}
