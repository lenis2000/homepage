{% raw %}<p>We apply the theorems from the theory of Toeplitz determinants to calculate the asymptotics of various correlators including the exponential ones in the inpenetrable one-dimensional Bose gas system. The known correlators in the free-fermion system are also used to test the generalized Fisher-Hartwig conjecture.</p>{% endraw %}
