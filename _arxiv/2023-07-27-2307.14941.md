{% raw %}<p>We study the speed of convergence to equilibrium for the asymmetric simple exclusion process (ASEP) on a finite interval with one open boundary. We provide sharp estimates on the total-variation distance from equilibrium and verify that the limit profile undergoes a phase transition from a Gaussian to a KPZ profile.</p>{% endraw %}
