{% raw %}<p>We consider exclusion processes on a rooted <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>d</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">d</annotation></semantics></math></span>-regular tree. We start from a Bernoulli product measure conditioned on having a particle at the root, which we call the tagged particle. For <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>d</mi><mo>≥</mo><mn>3</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">d\geq 3</annotation></semantics></math></span>, we show that the tagged particle has positive linear speed and satisfies a central limit theorem. We give an explicit formula for the speed. As a key step in the proof, we first show that the exclusion process "seen from the tagged particle" has an ergodic invariant measure.</p>{% endraw %}
