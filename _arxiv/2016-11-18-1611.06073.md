{% raw %}<p>We compute the limit shape for several classes of restricted integer partitions, where the restrictions are placed on the part sizes rather than the multiplicities. Our approach utilizes certain classes of bijections which map limit shapes continuously in the plane. We start with bijections outlined previously by the second author, and extend them to include limit shapes with different scaling functions.</p>{% endraw %}
