{% raw %}<p>We consider the bulk eigenvalue statistics of Laplacian matrices of large Erd\H{o}s-R\'enyi random graphs in the regime <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>p</mi><mo>≥</mo><msup><mi>N</mi><mi>δ</mi></msup><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">p \geq N^{\delta}/N</annotation></semantics></math></span> for any fixed <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>δ</mi><mo>&gt;</mo><mn>0</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\delta &gt;0</annotation></semantics></math></span>. We prove a local law down to the optimal scale <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>η</mi><mo>≳</mo><msup><mi>N</mi><mrow><mo>−</mo><mn>1</mn></mrow></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\eta \gtrsim N^{-1}</annotation></semantics></math></span> which implies that the eigenvectors are delocalized. We consider the local eigenvalue statistics and prove that both the gap statistics and averaged correlation functions coincide with the GOE in the bulk.</p>{% endraw %}
