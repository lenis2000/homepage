{% raw %}<p>We prove the exponential growth of the cardinality of the set of numbers of spanning trees in simple (and planar) graphs on <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>n</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">n</annotation></semantics></math></span> vertices, answering a question of Sedl\'a\v{c}ek from 1969. The proof uses a connection with continued fractions, ``thin orbits,'' and Zaremba's conjecture.</p>{% endraw %}
