{% raw %}<p>We prove a CLT for spectra of submatrices of real symmetric and Hermitian Wigner matrices. We show that if in the standard normalization the fourth moment of the off-digonal entries is GOE/GUE-like then the limiting Gaussian process can be viewed as a collection of simply yet nontrivially correlated two-dimensional Gaussian Free Fields.</p>{% endraw %}
