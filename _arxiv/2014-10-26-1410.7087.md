{% raw %}<p>We present a lower bound on the Kronecker coefficients for tensor squares of the symmetric group via the characters of~<span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>n</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">S_n</annotation></semantics></math></span>, which we apply to obtain various explicit estimates. Notably, we extend Sylvester's unimodality of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-binomial coefficients <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mrow><mo fence="true">(</mo><mfrac linethickness="0px"><mi>n</mi><mi>k</mi></mfrac><mo fence="true">)</mo></mrow><mi>q</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\binom{n}{k}_q</annotation></semantics></math></span> as polynomials in~<span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span> to derive sharp bounds on the differences of their consecutive coefficients. We then derive effective asymptotic lower bounds for a wider class of Kronecker coefficients.</p>{% endraw %}
