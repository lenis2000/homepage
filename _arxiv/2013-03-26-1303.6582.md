{% raw %}<p>We characterize all translation invariant half planar maps satisfying a certain natural domain Markov property. For p-angulations with p \ge 3 where all faces are simple, we show that these form a one-parameter family of measures H^{(p)}_{alpha}. For triangulations we also establish existence of a phase transition which affects many properties of these maps. The critical maps are the well known half plane uniform infinite planar maps. The sub-critical maps are identified as all possible limits of uniform measures on finite maps with given boundary and area.</p>{% endraw %}
