{% raw %}<p>We study some specializations and extensions of the quantum version of the MacMahon Master Theorem derived by Garoufalidis, Le and Zeilberger. In particular, we obtain a (t,q)-analogue for the Cartier-Foata noncommutative version and a semi-strong (t,q)-analogue for the contextual algebra.</p>{% endraw %}
