{% raw %}<p>Using an extension of the well-known evaluation symmetry, a new Cauchy-type identity for Macdonald polynomials is proved. After taking the classical limit this yields a new sl_3 generalisation of the famous Selberg integral. Closely related results obtained in this paper are an sl_3-analogue of the Askey-Habsieger-Kadell q-Selberg integral and an extension of the q-Selberg integral to a transformation between q-integrals of different dimensions.</p>{% endraw %}
