{% raw %}<p>We consider all totally asymmetric simple exclusion processes (TASEPs) whose transition probabilities are given in the Schütz-type formulas and which jump with homogeneous rates. We show that the multi-point distribution of particle positions and the coefficient of KPZ scaling are described using the probability generating function of the distribution followed when the rightmost particle jumps. For all TASEPs satisfying certain assumptions, We also prove the pointwise convergence of the kernels appearing in the joint distribution of particle positions to those appearing in the KPZ fixed point formula. Our result generalizes the result of Matetski, Quastel, and Remenik [Acta Math., 227, 115-203, (2021)].</p>{% endraw %}
