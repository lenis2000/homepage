{% raw %}<p>Baxterisation is a procedure which constructs solutions of the Yang-Baxter equation from algebra representations. A recent paper arXiv:2004.05035 provides Baxterisation formulas for a fused Hecke algebra. In this paper, we provide a stochastic version of Baxterisation for the fused Hecke algebra, which yields stochastic solutions to the Yang-Baxter equation. The coefficients in the Baxterisation formula previously appeared as the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q</annotation></semantics></math></span>-Hahn weights. This results in new formulas for the weights of the stochastic higher-spin vertex model.</p>{% endraw %}
