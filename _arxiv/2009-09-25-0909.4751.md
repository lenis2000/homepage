{% raw %}<p>This is a historical note. In 1993 we calculated space, time and temperature dependent correlation function in isotropic version of one dimensional XY spin chain. The correlation function decays exponentially with time and space separation. The rate of exponential decay was evaluated explicitly. Since that time similar results were obtained in other models: Bose gas with delta interaction, Ising model and strongly correlated electrons.</p>{% endraw %}
