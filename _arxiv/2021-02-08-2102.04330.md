{% raw %}<p>We show that the fluctuations of the largest eigenvalue of a real symmetric or complex Hermitian Wigner matrix of size <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span> converge to the Tracy--Widom laws at a rate <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>O</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msup><mi>N</mi><mrow><mo>−</mo><mn>1</mn><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mn>3</mn><mo>+</mo><mi>ω</mi></mrow></msup><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">O(N^{-1/3+\omega})</annotation></semantics></math></span>, as <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span> tends to infinity. For Wigner matrices this improves the previous rate <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>O</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msup><mi>N</mi><mrow><mo>−</mo><mn>2</mn><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mn>9</mn><mo>+</mo><mi>ω</mi></mrow></msup><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">O(N^{-2/9+\omega})</annotation></semantics></math></span> obtained by Bourgade [5] for generalized Wigner matrices. Our result follows from a Green function comparison theorem, originally introduced by Erd\H{o}s, Yau and Yin [19] to prove edge universality, on a finer spectral parameter scale with improved error estimates. The proof relies on the continuous Green function flow induced by a matrix-valued Ornstein--Uhlenbeck process. Precise estimates on leading contributions from the third and fourth order moments of the matrix entries are obtained using iterative cumulant expansions and recursive comparisons for correlation functions, along with uniform convergence estimates for correlation kernels of the Gaussian invariant ensembles.</p>{% endraw %}
