About

Since 2014 I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Mathematics, University of Virginia. I got my PhD in 2010 in Moscow with Grigori Olshanski, and in 2011-2014 was a postdoc at Northeastern University.

Research interests • (papers)

Integrable probability, KPZ universality, Yang-Baxter equation, Bethe ansatz, Macdonald processes, symmetric functions, random tilings.

Teaching • (all)

In academic year 2017-18 I am on sabbatical at MIT and do not teach at University of Virginia.
