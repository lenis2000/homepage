Universal Barcode Process

Paths in the transversal direction form a "barcode"

Conjecture [Knizel-P. 2025]
The 1D local statistics in the transversal direction converge to a universal barcode process
Predicted density:
\(\displaystyle \rho(t) = \mathfrak{a}\left(1-\frac{t}{2}\right) \sum_{n \in \frac{1}{2}\mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}} \frac{-q^{-n} \mathcal{F}_n^t(0) \mathcal{F}_n^{t-1}(0)}{\|\mathcal{F}_n\|_{\ell^2(\mathbb{Z})}^2}\)
Key ingredient:
Eigenfunctions of limiting inter-slice operators:
\(\mathcal{F}_n(x)\) involves theta functions and q-factorials
