A Natural Deformation: q-Racah Weights

Idea:
Deform the uniform measure by weighting each horizontal lozenge at position \(j\):
Two parameters:
\(q \in (0,1)\) — overall tilt
\(\kappa > 0\) — deformation strength
Key property:
\(\kappa \to 0\): becomes \(q^{-\text{vol}}\)
\(\kappa \to \infty\): becomes \(q^{+\text{vol}}\)
Why "q-Racah"?
Named after the orthogonal polynomials that diagonalize this model — at the top of the classical hierarchy [Borodin–Gorin–Rains 2009]

Lozenges ↔ noncolliding paths \(x_1(t) < x_2(t) < \cdots < x_N(t)\)

Weight of a tiling:
\[\mathbb{P}(\text{tiling}) = \frac{1}{Z} \prod_{\text{horiz. lozenges}} \mathrm{w}_{q,\kappa}(j), \qquad \mathrm{w}_{q,\kappa}(j) = \kappa q^{j-\frac{S+1}{2}} - \frac{1}{\kappa q^{j-\frac{S+1}{2}}}\]
