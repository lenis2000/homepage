q-Racah Measure on Lozenge Tilings

Orthogonal polynomials:
Uniform ↔ Hahn
q-volume ↔ q-Hahn
q-Racah ↔ q-Racah poly.
Key property:
\(\kappa \to 0\): becomes \(q^{-\text{vol}}\)
\(\kappa \to \infty\): becomes \(q^{+\text{vol}}\)

Lozenges ↔ noncolliding paths \(x_1(t) < x_2(t) < \cdots < x_N(t)\)

Probability measure:
\[\mathbb{P}(\text{tiling}) = \frac{1}{Z} \prod_{\text{horiz. lozenges}} \mathrm{w}_{q,\kappa}(j), \qquad \mathrm{w}_{q,\kappa}(j) = \kappa q^{j-\frac{S+1}{2}} - \frac{1}{\kappa q^{j-\frac{S+1}{2}}}, \qquad j = x - \tfrac{t}{2} + 1\]
References: [Borodin-Gorin-Rains 2009], [Dimitrov-Knizel 2019], [Gorin-Huang 2022], [Morales-Pak-Panova 2019]
