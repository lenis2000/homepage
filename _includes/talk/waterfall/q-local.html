Local Universality for \(q = e^{-\gamma/N} \approx 1\): still IID Bernoulli!
For \(q\) close to \(1\), local structure remains independent Bernoulli trials.
The typical path (global limit shape) maximizes entropy minus \(\gamma \times\) area [Vershik 1996]. Here, entropy comes from the number of discrete paths approximating a given curve. This yields IID Bernoulli with position-dependent \(p\) = slope of the limit shape.
