Local structure is IID Bernoulli: At each step, choose R with prob. \(\frac{a}{a+b}\), U with prob. \(\frac{b}{a+b}\) — independent trials!
Law of Large Numbers: Path follows the diagonal — this is the limit shape phenomenon. What are the fluctuations?
Question: What does the histogram of the midpoint fluctuations resemble?
Central Limit Theorem: The midpoint distribution is the Bell curve (Gaussian). The whole path minus the diagonal converges to Brownian bridge.
