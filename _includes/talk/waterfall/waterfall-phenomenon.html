The Waterfall Phenomenon

q = 0.8, κ = i·3

Fixed q with imaginary κ → "Waterfall" surface

Parameters:
\(q \in (0,1)\) fixed (e.g., \(q = 0.8\))
\(\kappa = i \cdot c\) purely imaginary
Observation [BGR 2009]
The limit shape becomes flat but NOT parallel to any coordinate plane
Path slopes in the bulk:
0, 1, and 1/2
(The new slope 1/2 is the "waterfall")
