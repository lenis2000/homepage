Height Fluctuations

Gaussian Free Field
Like the Brownian bridge, but with two-dimensional time
\(\displaystyle \text{GFF} \approx \frac{h_1 - h_2}{\sqrt{2}}\)
where \(h_1, h_2\) are two independent samples of the height function.
