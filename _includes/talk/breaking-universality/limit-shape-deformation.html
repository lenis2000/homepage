Limit Shapes Under Random Weights

Law of Large Numbers [Bufetov–P.–Zografos 2025]:
The random height function concentrates around a deterministic limit shape, which depends on the distribution of the edge weights
Deformed arctic curve:
The frozen boundary is no longer a circle — it deforms according to the weight distribution. The arctic curve is still algebraic (for explicit weight distributions)
Self-averaging:
The limit shape is the same for almost every realization of the random weights (quenched = annealed at the LLN level)
