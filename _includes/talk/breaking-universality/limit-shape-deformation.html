Limit Shapes Under Random Weights

Limit shape equation:
Solve for \(z\) in the upper half-plane:
\(\displaystyle \frac{z}{z-1} + az \, \mathbf{E}_{\mathcal{B}} \frac{\beta}{1-\beta+\beta z} = \alpha^{-1} y\)
Limiting density:
\(\displaystyle (\alpha,y)\mapsto \frac{1}{\pi}\,\mathrm{Arg}\bigl(z(\alpha,y)\bigr)\)
Arctic curve: locus of double roots in \(z\). Has an exact parametrization.
