Disordered Dimer Models: 2025 Landscape

Straight-layered weights on the Aztec diamond [Moulard–Toninelli 2025]
Horizontal (not diagonal) layered i.i.d. weights. Different algebraic structure (free probability), but similar phenomenology: LLN with explicit limit shapes, CLT with Brownian motion component.
Gamma-distributed edge weights [Duits–Van Peski 2025]
All edges carry i.i.d. Gamma-distributed weights. Connections to random matrix products and Lyapunov exponents. Determinantal structure via biorthogonal ensembles.
Fluctuation universality for layered weights [Zografos 2025]
Extends the CLT results of [Bufetov–P.–Zografos] to more general weight distributions, establishing universality of the Brownian motion fluctuations.
Common themes: disorder breaks GFF universality; new universal objects emerge (Brownian motion, Lyapunov exponents); exact solvability persists through different algebraic mechanisms
