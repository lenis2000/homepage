(B)  A second mechanism: random edge weights

Setup. Aztec diamond, domino tilings — GFF universality holds in the deterministic case [Kenyon 2001], [Bufetov–Knizel 2018], [Chelkak–Laslier–Russkikh 2020], [Berggren–Nicoletti–Russkikh 2024].
Question. What breaks if the edge weights are themselves random?
Two regimes [Bufetov–P.–Zografos 2025]
  1. Critical vanishing variance \(\operatorname{Var}[W_j] \sim 1/M\): same limit shape, fluctuations on scale \(\sqrt{\log M}\) — GFF + Brownian motion
  2. Fixed distribution independent of \(M\): new limit shape, fluctuations on scale \(\sqrt{M}\) — Brownian motion only
