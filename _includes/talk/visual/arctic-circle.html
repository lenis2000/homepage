Frozen vs Liquid Regions

Frozen corners:
Near the edges of the box, the tiling is forced — all lozenges point the same way. No randomness here.
Liquid center:
In the middle, the tiling is random — all three types of lozenges appear, and the surface looks smooth.
Arctic boundary:
A sharp curve separates the frozen and liquid regions. In the hexagon, this boundary is an inscribed ellipse. [Jockusch–Propp–Shor 1998], [Cohn–Larsen–Propp 1998]
