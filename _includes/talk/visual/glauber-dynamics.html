Glauber Dynamics

Elementary move:
Three lozenges around a hexagon can be flipped — rotated 60°. This is the only local move that preserves the tiling.
Markov chain:
  • Pick a random hexagon
  • If it's flippable, flip it
  • Repeat many times
After enough steps, the tiling is approximately random (close to equilibrium).
How fast?
For the hexagon, Glauber dynamics mixes in \(O(N^{2+o(1)})\) steps. [Laslier–Toninelli 2015], [Caputo–Martinelli–Toninelli 2012]
