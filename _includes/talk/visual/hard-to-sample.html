When Sampling is Hard

CFTP can be slow:
When the model has periodic weights or non-uniform boundary conditions, the monotone sandwich may take exponentially long to collapse.
Why?
Certain configurations act as bottlenecks — the Markov chain gets stuck. The maximal and minimal chains refuse to agree on the last few steps.
Alternative approaches:
  • Algebraic exact sampling [Borodin–Gorin–Rains 2009]
  • Schur process sampling
  • Determinantal point processes
