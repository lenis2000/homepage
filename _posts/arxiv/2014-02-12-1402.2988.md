{% raw %}<p>We consider a one dimensional infinite chain of har- monic oscillators whose dynamics is perturbed by a stochastic term conserving energy and momentum. We prove that in the unpinned case the macroscopic evolution of the energy converges to a fractional diffusion. For a pinned system we prove that energy evolves diffusively, generalizing some of the results of [4].</p>{% endraw %}
