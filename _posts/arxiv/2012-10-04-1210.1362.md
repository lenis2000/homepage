{% raw %}<p>Let "mu" be a point process on a countable discrete space "X". Under assumption that "mu" is quasi-invariant with respect to any finitary permutation of "X", we describe a general scheme for constructing an equilibrium Kawasaki dynamics for which "mu" is a symmetrizing (and hence invariant) measure. We also exhibit a two-parameter family of point processes "mu" possessing the needed quasi-invariance property. Each process of this family is determinantal, and its correlation kernel is the kernel of a projection operator in the Hilbert space of square-summable functions on "X".</p>{% endraw %}
