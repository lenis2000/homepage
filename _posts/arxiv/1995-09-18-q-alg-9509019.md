{% raw %}<p>In this paper we apply the method of psi-vectors to three dimensional statistical models. This method gives the correspondence between the Bazhanov -- Baxter model and its vertex formulation. Considering psi-vectors for the Planar model, we obtain its self-duality.</p>{% endraw %}
