{% raw %}<p>We find and prove a factorization formula for certain Macdonald Littlewood-Richardson coefficients $c_{\lambda \mu}^\nu(q,t)$. Namely, we consider the case that the Kostka number $K_{\mu, \nu-\lambda}$ is $1$. This settles a particular case of a more general conjecture of Richard Stanely. This conjecture proposes that a factorization formula exists whenever the corresponding regular Littlewood-Richardson coefficient $c^\nu_{\lambda \mu}$ is $1$.</p>{% endraw %}
