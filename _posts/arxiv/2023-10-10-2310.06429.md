{% raw %}We discuss how to construct limit shapes for the domino tiling model (square lattice dimer model) and $5$-vertex model, in appropriate polygonal domains. Our methods are based on the harmonic extension method of [R. Kenyon and I. Prause, Gradient variational problems in $\mathbb{R}^2$, Duke Math J. 2022].{% endraw %}
