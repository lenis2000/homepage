{% raw %}<p>We give a new derivation of the finite <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>N</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">N</annotation></semantics></math></span> master loop equation for lattice Yang-Mills theory with structure group <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>O</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">SO(N)</annotation></semantics></math></span>, <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>U</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">U(N)</annotation></semantics></math></span> or <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>U</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">SU(N)</annotation></semantics></math></span>. The <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>O</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">SO(N)</annotation></semantics></math></span> case was initially proved by Chatterjee in \cite{Cha}, and <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>S</mi><mi>U</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">SU(N)</annotation></semantics></math></span> was analyzed in a follow-up work by Jafarov \cite{Jafar}. Our approach is based on the Langevin dynamic, an SDE on the manifold of configurations, and yields a simple proof via It\^o's formula.</p>{% endraw %}
