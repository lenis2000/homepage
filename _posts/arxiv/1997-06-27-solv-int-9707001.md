{% raw %}<p>The focus of this survey paper is on the distribution function for the largest eigenvalue in the finite N Gaussian ensembles (GOE,GUE,GSE) in the edge scaling limit of N->infinity. These limiting distribution functions are expressible in terms of a particular Painleve II function. Comparisons are made with finite N simulations as well as a discussion of the universality of these distribution functions.</p>{% endraw %}
