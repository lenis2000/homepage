{% raw %}<p>The estimation of large covariance matrices has a high dimensional bias. Correcting for this bias can be reformulated via the tool of Free Probability Theory as a free deconvolution. The goal of this work is a computational and statistical resolution of this problem. Our approach is based on complex-analytic methods methods to invert $S$-transforms. In particular, one needs a theoretical understanding of the Riemann surfaces where multivalued $S$ transforms live and an efficient computational scheme.</p>{% endraw %}
