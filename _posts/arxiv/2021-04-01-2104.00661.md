{% raw %}<p>We consider the asymmetric simple exclusion process (ASEP) on $\mathbb{Z}$ started from step initial data and obtain the exact Lyapunov exponents for $H_0(t)$, the integrated current of ASEP. As a corollary, we derive an explicit formula for the upper-tail large deviation rate function for $-H_0(t)$. Our result matches with the rate function for the integrated current of the totally asymmetric simple exclusion process (TASEP) obtained in [Johansson 00](arXiv:math/9903134).</p>{% endraw %}
