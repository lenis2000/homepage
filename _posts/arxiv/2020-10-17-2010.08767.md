When the moment generating functions are bounded in an interval around the origin, this probability can be bounded below by <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>1</mn><mo>−</mo><mi>O</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>x</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>μ</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><mi>N</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">1-O(x|\mu| \log N)</annotation></semantics></math></span>.
