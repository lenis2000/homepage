{% raw %}<p>We prove that the boundary of the Hall-Littlewood <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>t</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">t</annotation></semantics></math></span>-deformation of the Gelfand-Tsetlin graph is parametrized by infinite integer signatures, extending results of Gorin and Cuenca on boundaries of related deformed Gelfand-Tsetlin graphs. In the special case when <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>1</mn><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mi>t</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">1/t</annotation></semantics></math></span> is a prime <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>p</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">p</annotation></semantics></math></span> we use this to recover results of Bufetov-Qiu and Assiotis on infinite <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>p</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">p</annotation></semantics></math></span>-adic random matrices, placing them in the general context of branching graphs derived from symmetric functions. Our methods rely on explicit formulas for certain skew Hall-Littlewood polynomials. As a separate corollary to these, we obtain a simple expression for the joint distribution of the cokernels of products <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>2</mn></msub><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo separator="true">,</mo><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>3</mn></msub><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>2</mn></msub><msub><mi>A</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo separator="true">,</mo><mo>…</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A_1, A_2A_1, A_3A_2A_1,\ldots</annotation></semantics></math></span> of independent Haar-distributed matrices <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>A</mi><mi>i</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A_i</annotation></semantics></math></span> over the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>p</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">p</annotation></semantics></math></span>-adic integers <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="double-struck">Z</mi><mi>p</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\mathbb{Z}_p</annotation></semantics></math></span>.
