{% raw %}We give a short overview of recent developments in exact solutions for macroscopic fluctuation theory by using connections to classical integrable systems. A calculation of the cumulant generating function for a tagged particle is also given, agreeing with a previous result obtained from a microscopic analysis.{% endraw %}
