Via a coupling technique we derive limiting laws of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>X</mi><mi>n</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">X_n</annotation></semantics></math></span>, using previous results on regenerative compositions derived from stick-breaking partitions of the unit interval.
