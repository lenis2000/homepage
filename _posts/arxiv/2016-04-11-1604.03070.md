{% raw %}The Muttalib-Borodin biorthogonal ensemble is a joint density function for $n$ particles on the positive real line that depends on a parameter $θ$. There is an equilibrium problem that describes the large $n$ behavior. We show that for rational values of $θ$ there is an equivalent vector equilibrium problem.{% endraw %}
