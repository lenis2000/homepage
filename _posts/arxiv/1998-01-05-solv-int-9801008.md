{% raw %}<p>In this expository article we describe the asymptotics of certain Fredholm determinants which provide solutions to the cylindrical Toda equations, and we explain how these asymptotics are derived. The connection with Fredholm determinants arising in the theory of random matrices, and their asymptotics, are also discussed.</p>{% endraw %}
