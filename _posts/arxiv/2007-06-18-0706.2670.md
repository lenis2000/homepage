{% raw %}<p>We give a closed form for the correlation functions of ensembles of asymmetric real matrices in terms of the Pfaffian of an antisymmetric matrix formed from a <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>2</mn><mo>×</mo><mn>2</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">2 \times 2</annotation></semantics></math></span> matrix kernel associated to the ensemble. We also derive closed forms for the matrix kernel and correlation functions for Ginibre's real ensemble.</p>{% endraw %}
