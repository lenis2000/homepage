{% raw %}We provide the first construction of stationary measures for the open KPZ equation on the spatial interval $[0,1]$ with general inhomogeneous Neumann boundary conditions at $0$ and $1$ depending on real parameters $u$ and $v$, respectively. When $u+v\geq 0$ we uniquely characterize the constructed stationary measures through their multipoint Laplace transform which we prove is given in terms of a stochastic process that we call the continuous dual Hahn process.{% endraw %}
