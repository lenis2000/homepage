{% raw %}<p>We prove that the interface of critical site percolation on the triangular lattice converges to SLE$_6$ in its natural parametrization, where the discrete interface is parametrized such that each edge is crossed in one unit of time, while the limiting curve is parametrized by the $7/4$-dimensional Minkowski content. We also prove that the scaling limit of counting measure on the pivotal points, which was proved to exist by Garban, Pete, and Schramm (2013), is the $3/4$-dimensional Minkowski content up to a deterministic multiplicative constant.</p>{% endraw %}
