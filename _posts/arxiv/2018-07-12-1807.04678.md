{% raw %}<p>In this article we study the stochastic six vertex model under the scaling proposed by Borodin and Gorin (2018), where the weights of corner-shape vertices are tuned to zero, and prove Conjecture 6.1 therein: that the height fluctuation converges in finite dimensional distributions to the solution of stochastic telegraph equation.</p>{% endraw %}
