{% raw %}<p>We derive the large-N, all order asymptotic expansion for a system of N particles with mean-field interactions on top of a Coulomb repulsion at temperature 1/\beta, under the assumptions that the interactions are analytic, off-critical, and satisfy a local strict convexity assumption.</p>{% endraw %}
