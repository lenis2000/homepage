{% raw %}We give contour integral formulas for the generating function of the joint distribution of the PushASEP on a ring. We obtained these formulas through a rigorous treatment of Bethe Ansatz. The approach relies on residue computations and controlling the location of the Bethe roots, which we achieve by partially decoupling the Bethe equations through extending the system of equations. Moreover, we are able to use our formulas to compute the asymptotic fluctuations for the flat and step initial conditions at the relaxation time scale.{% endraw %}
