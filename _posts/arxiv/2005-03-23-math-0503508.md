{% raw %}<p>We study random skew 3D partitions weighted by <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msup><mi>q</mi><mtext>vol</mtext></msup></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q^{\textup{vol}}</annotation></semantics></math></span> and, specifically, the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>q</mi><mo>→</mo><mn>1</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">q\to 1</annotation></semantics></math></span> asymptotics of local correlations near various points of the limit shape.
