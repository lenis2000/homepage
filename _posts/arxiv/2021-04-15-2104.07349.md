Our results support the validity of the proposed criterion of Liouvillian <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi mathvariant="script">P</mi><mi mathvariant="script">T</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\mathcal{PT}</annotation></semantics></math></span> symmetry.</p>{% endraw %}
