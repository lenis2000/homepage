{% raw %}The Ferrari-Spohn diffusion process arises as limit process for the 2D Ising model as well as random walks with area penalty. Motivated by the 3D Ising model, we consider $M$ such diffusions conditioned not to intersect. We show that the top process converges to the Airy$_2$ process as $M\to\infty$. We then explain the relation with the 3D Ising model and present some conjectures about it.{% endraw %}
