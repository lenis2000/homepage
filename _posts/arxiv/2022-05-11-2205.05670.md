The <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mn>2</mn><mi mathvariant="normal">/</mi><mn>3</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">2/3</annotation></semantics></math></span> exponent is tight such that the one-point distribution of the rescaled paths converges to the geodesics of the directed landscape.
