{% raw %}<p>We introduce families of maximal commutative subalgebras, called Bethe subalgebras, of the group algebra of the symmetric group. Bethe subalgebras are deformations of the Gelfand-Zetlin subalgebra. We describe various properties of Bethe subalgebras.</p>{% endraw %}
