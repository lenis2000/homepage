{% raw %}<p>Various best-choice problems related to the planar homogeneous Poisson process in finite or semi-infinite rectangle are studied. The analysis is largely based on properties of the one-dimensional box-area process associated with the sequence of records. We prove a series of distributional identities involving exponential and uniform random variables, and resolve the Petruccelli-Porosinski-Samuels paradox on coincidence of asymptotic values in certain discrete-time optimal stopping problems.</p>{% endraw %}
