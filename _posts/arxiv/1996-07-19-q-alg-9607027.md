{% raw %}<p>The spectral decomposition of the path space of the vertex model associated to the vector representation of the quantized affine algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>U</mi><mi>q</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mover accent="true"><mrow><mi>s</mi><mi>l</mi></mrow><mo>^</mo></mover><mi>n</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">U_q(\hat{sl}_n)</annotation></semantics></math></span> is studied. We give a one-to-one correspondence between the spin configurations and the semi-standard tableaux of skew Young diagrams. As a result we obtain a formula of the characters for the degeneracy of the spectrum in terms of skew Schur functions. We conjecture that our result describes the <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>s</mi><msub><mi>l</mi><mi>n</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">sl_n</annotation></semantics></math></span>-module contents of the Yangian <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>Y</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><msub><mi>l</mi><mi>n</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">Y(sl_n)</annotation></semantics></math></span>-module structures of the level 1 integrable modules of the affine Lie algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mover accent="true"><mrow><mi>s</mi><mi>l</mi></mrow><mo>^</mo></mover><mi>n</mi></msub></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\hat{sl}_n</annotation></semantics></math></span>. An analogous result is obtained also for a vertex model associated to the quantized twisted affine algebra <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>U</mi><mi>q</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msubsup><mi>A</mi><mrow><mn>2</mn><mi>n</mi></mrow><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mn>2</mn><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow></msubsup><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">U_q(A^{(2)}_{2n})</annotation></semantics></math></span>, where <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>Y</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><msub><mi>B</mi><mi>n</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">Y(B_n)</annotation></semantics></math></span> characters appear for the degeneracy of the spectrum. The relation to the spectrum of the Haldane-Shastry and the Polychronakos models are also discussed.</p>{% endraw %}
