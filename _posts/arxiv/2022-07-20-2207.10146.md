{% raw %}In 2015, Vladimir Fock proved that the spectral transform, associating to an element of a dimer cluster integrable system its spectral data, is birational by constructing an inverse map using theta functions on Jacobians of spectral curves. We provide an alternate construction of the inverse map that involves only rational functions in the spectral data.{% endraw %}
