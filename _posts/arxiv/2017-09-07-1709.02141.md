Applied to Continuous Time Random Walk(CTRW) \'a la Montroll and Wiess we show that CTRW with renewal times in a weakly dense set of <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>D</mi><mi>O</mi><mi>A</mi><mrow><mo fence="true">(</mo><mi>α</mi><mo fence="true">)</mo></mrow></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">DOA\left(\alpha\right)</annotation></semantics></math></span> can be realized as random walk in a random environment. We find the quenched limit and give a bound on the error of the approximation.</p>{% endraw %}
