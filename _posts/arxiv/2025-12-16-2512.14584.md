{% raw %}<p>We study the asymmetric simple exclusion process (ASEP) on a segment $\{1,\ldots,b_N\}$ and are interested in its total variation distance to equilibrium when started from an initial configuration $\xi^{N}$. We provide a general result which gives the cutoff window and profile whenever a KPZ-type limit theorem is available for an extension of $\xi^{N}$ to $\mathbb{Z}$. We apply this result to obtain the cutoff window and profile of ASEP on the segment with flat, half-flat and step initial data. Our arguments are entirely probabilistic and make no use of Hecke algebras.</p>{% endraw %}
