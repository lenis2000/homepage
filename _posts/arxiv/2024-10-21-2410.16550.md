{% raw %}We extend the construction of the semigroup of the two-dimensional delta-Bose gas in Gu, Quastel, and Tsai (2021) (based on Rajeev (1999) and Dimock and Rajeev (2004)) to a weighted $L^2$ space that allows exponentially growing functions. We further show that the semigroup of the mollified delta-Bose gas converges strongly to that of the delta-Bose gas.{% endraw %}
