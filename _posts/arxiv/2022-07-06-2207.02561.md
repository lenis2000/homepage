{% raw %}<p>We resolve two open problems on Kronecker coefficients $g(λ,μ,ν)$ of the symmetric group. First, we prove that for partitions $λ,μ,ν$ with fixed Durfee square size, the Kronecker coefficients grow at most polynomially. Second, we show that the maximal Kronecker coefficients $g(λ,λ,λ)$ for self-conjugate partitions $λ$ grow superexponentially. We also give applications to explicit special cases.</p>{% endraw %}
