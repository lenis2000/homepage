{% raw %}<p>A previous paper of the author developed a general method for producing explicit central elements of quantized Lie algebras using Lusztig's inner product. This method had previously been applied for the type $C_2$, $D_3$ and $D_4$ Lie algebras. The current paper repeats the calculation for the type $B_2$ Lie algebra, which is actually isomorphic to the $C_2$ Lie algebra. The explicit expression for the corresponding quantum Hamiltonian is computed.</p>{% endraw %}
