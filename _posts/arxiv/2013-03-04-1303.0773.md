{% raw %}<p>In this review, we investigate a key information-theoretic performance metric in multiple-antenna wireless communications, the so-called outage probability. This quantity may be expressed in terms of a moment generating function, for which we present two separate integral representations, one involving a particular <span class="katex"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>σ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\sigma</annotation></semantics></math></span>-form of Painlev\'e V. We also present a representation involving a non-linear second order difference equation.</p>{% endraw %}
