{% raw %}<p>This is a review (including some background material) of the author's work and related activity on certain exactly solvable statistical models in two dimensions, including the six-vertex model, loop models and lozenge tilings. Applications to enumerative combinatorics and to algebraic geometry are described.</p>{% endraw %}
